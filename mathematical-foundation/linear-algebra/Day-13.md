# 📘 Week 6 — Day 13: Eigenvalues and Eigenvectors (Intuition)

## Objective

Today's objective was to develop an intuitive understanding of **eigenvalues** and **eigenvectors** without focusing on the mathematical derivations.

The goal is to understand why these concepts are fundamental to dimensionality reduction techniques such as PCA and many machine learning algorithms.

---

## What I Learned

* A matrix usually changes both the length and direction of a vector.
* Some special vectors do **not** change their direction after the transformation.
* These special vectors are called **eigenvectors**.
* The amount by which they are stretched or compressed is called the **eigenvalue**.

---

## Why It Matters

Eigenvalues and eigenvectors form the mathematical foundation for:

* Principal Component Analysis (PCA)
* Dimensionality Reduction
* Image Compression
* Recommendation Systems
* Signal Processing
* Machine Learning

Although modern AI engineers rarely compute eigenvalues manually, understanding their intuition helps explain how data can be compressed while preserving important information.
