# Why Probability Matters in AI

Artificial Intelligence rarely works with certainty. Instead, AI models estimate the likelihood of different outcomes based on available data.

Probability provides the mathematical framework for handling uncertainty and making predictions.

Many AI applications—including spam detection, recommendation systems, speech recognition, computer vision, and Large Language Models—rely on probabilistic reasoning.
